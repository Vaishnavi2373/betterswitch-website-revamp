## 1. Who We Are

BetterSwitch is a B2B Payment Systems as a Service (PSaaS) company that helps businesses deploy PCI-compliant payment systems, including Orchestrators, Merchant of Record systems, Facilitators, and Gateways. We provide:

- Payment system deployment on your infrastructure
- PCI DSS compliance consulting
- Payment processor integration services
- Analytics and dashboard solutions
- Ongoing maintenance and support

**Important Note**: Because our solution is self-hosted on your cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or Azure), payment data processed through your deployment is controlled by you, not by BetterSwitch.

## 5. Data Processed Through Your Deployment

### 5.1 Self-Hosted Architecture
A critical distinction: the payment infrastructure we deploy is **self-hosted on your cloud environment**. This means:

- **You are the data controller** for all payment and customer data
- Payment card data is tokenized and stored within your infrastructure
- Transaction data remains in your systems
- BetterSwitch does not have persistent access to this data

### 5.2 Support Access
When you request support, you may grant us temporary access to troubleshoot issues. This access:
- Is limited in scope and duration
- Is logged for audit purposes
- Does not involve exporting or retaining your customer data
- Is revoked upon completion of support activities

### 5.3 Your Compliance Obligations
As the data controller, you are responsible for:
- Your own privacy policy for your customers
- Compliance with PCI DSS for payment data
- Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable regulations
- Data subject requests from your customers
