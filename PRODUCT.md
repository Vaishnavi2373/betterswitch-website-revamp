# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Payment businesses, fintech founders, banks, and enterprise teams who want to own and operate their own payment system without building one from scratch. They range from startups wanting a branded payment orchestrator to established financial institutions needing a white-labelled facilitator or acquirer. Their context: they understand payments deeply and want full control, branding, and ownership of the payment experience they offer to their customers.

## Product Purpose

BetterSwitch is Payment Systems as a Service (PSaaS). Unlike single payment providers (Stripe, Adyen), BetterSwitch is the layer above: clients use our white-labelled SaaS platform to deploy and operate their own fully branded payment systems. They can spin up Orchestrators, Merchant of Record systems, Facilitators, Gateways, or hybrid configurations on demand.

### Core Platform

- **200+ payment connectors** (Stripe, Adyen, PayPal, Klarna, Razorpay, Checkout.com, and more)
- **Smart routing engine** with automatic failover, retries, and cost-based optimization
- **PCI DSS Level 1 compliance** built in from day one
- **White-label dashboards** with full branding customization
- **Storefronts and payment links** for direct commerce
- **E-commerce plugins** (WooCommerce, Shopify)
- **Multi-cloud deployment** (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- **KYC/KYB workflows** for merchant onboarding
- **Audit logs** for compliance tracking

### AI Sales Agent

BetterSwitch includes an AI-powered sales agent that helps clients (payment companies built on BetterSwitch) find and acquire their own merchants and customers. This is a key differentiator: not only do we help you launch a payment company, we help you grow it.

### Four System Types

1. **Orchestrator**: Route transactions across 200+ processors with smart routing, automatic failover, and cost optimization.
2. **Merchant of Record (MOR)**: Handle payments on behalf of merchants. Take on the compliance burden so sellers can focus on selling.
3. **Facilitator**: Power payments for marketplaces and platforms. Sub-merchant management, split payments, and regulatory compliance.
4. **Gateway**: Direct processor connectivity with tokenization, 3DS authentication, and PCI scope reduction.

### Pricing Model

One-time setup fee (custom based on system type) + per-transaction cost (volume-based pricing). No hidden fees.

### Why It Exists

The payments industry is fragmenting: every vertical, region, and business model needs its own payment stack, but nobody should have to build one from zero. BetterSwitch provides the infrastructure so clients can focus on their business.

### Success

A client goes from zero to a live, PCI-compliant, fully branded payment system in a day, with smart routing across 200+ processors, complete white-labelling, and ongoing dedicated engineering support.

## Brand Personality

Infrastructural confidence. Precise, capable, understated.

Three words: **Authoritative. Engineered. Clear.**

The brand should feel like the company that builds the machines that build the machines. Not flashy fintech, not legacy banking. The tone is a senior engineer explaining architecture at a whiteboard: direct, specific, no filler. Stripe's clarity is the baseline, but BetterSwitch operates one abstraction layer higher, so the communication should convey platform-scale thinking, not single-product simplicity.

## Anti-references

- **Generic crypto/web3 aesthetic**: dark gradients, neon accents, blockchain jargon, "decentralized everything." BetterSwitch is payments infrastructure, not DeFi.
- **Corporate banking blue**: stiff, conservative, stock-photo-driven enterprise sites (FIS, Fiserv).
- **Overly playful fintech**: cartoon illustrations, pastel gradients, "money made fun" energy (Cash App, Revolut consumer).
- **Dashboard-screenshot marketing**: hero sections that are just a blurred screenshot of the product with a gradient overlay.
- **Template SaaS cream**: the beige/cream/soft-gradient aesthetic that every AI SaaS startup adopted in 2024. Warm neutrals with pill buttons and sans-serif. Indistinguishable.

## Design Principles

1. **Platform, not product**: Every design choice should communicate that BetterSwitch is infrastructure, not an end-user app. Show the system, not a single screen.
2. **Earned complexity**: The product is genuinely complex (multi-tenant, multi-rail, compliance-aware). Don't hide that. Reveal it progressively with clarity, not simplification.
3. **Precision over decoration**: Every element should communicate something. No decorative gradients, no ornamental animations. If it moves, it means something.
4. **Show the multiplier**: The key differentiator is "one platform, many payment systems." Design should reinforce this 1-to-many relationship visually.
5. **Trust through craft**: Payment infrastructure demands trust. That trust comes from meticulous typography, consistent spacing, and zero rough edges, not from trust badges and compliance logos.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum across all surfaces.
- High contrast ratios for all text, especially on dark backgrounds where the current site is weakest.
- Reduced motion support: all animations must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Color-blind safe: never rely on color alone to convey status or meaning.
- Keyboard navigable: all interactive elements must be reachable and operable via keyboard.
